<p><ul><li>Desktop Type</li><li>Integrated Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PCB prototyping machines are primarily categorized into two market types: desktop type and integrated type. Desktop type machines are compact, user-friendly, and designed for small-scale, individual use, often favored by hobbyists and small businesses for quick prototyping. In contrast, integrated type machines are larger, high-capacity systems that combine multiple functions—such as milling, drilling, and etching—into one unit, catering to professional environments and higher production demands. Both types facilitate rapid prototyping, enabling faster design iterations and product development.</p></p>
