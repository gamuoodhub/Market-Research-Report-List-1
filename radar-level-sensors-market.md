<p><strong>The Radar Level Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through-Air</li><li>Guided-Wave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radar level sensors are categorized mainly into two types: Through-Air and Guided-Wave. Through-Air radar sensors utilize electromagnetic waves to measure the distance to a surface, enabling level detection in various materials without physical contact. This type excels in challenging environments. Guided-Wave radar sensors, on the other hand, use a probe to guide microwaves along its length, providing accurate level measurement even in harsh conditions. Both types are pivotal in industries such as chemical, water treatment, and food processing for monitoring liquid and solid levels.</p></p>
