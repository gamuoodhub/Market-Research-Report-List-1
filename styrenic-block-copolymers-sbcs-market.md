<p><p>Styrenic Block Copolymers (SBCs) are versatile thermoplastic elastomers made from linear or branched block copolymers that combine polystyrene with rubbery mid-blocks. The primary types include Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene (SBS), known for its excellent elasticity; Styrene-Isoprene-Styrene (SIS), valued for its flexibility; and Styrene-Ethylene-Butylene-Styrene (SEBS), which offers superior thermal stability and chemical resistance. Other SBC types include styrenic copolymers for specialized applications. The SBC market serves industries like automotive, adhesives, and consumer goods, driven by their unique physical properties and processing advantages.</p></p>
