<p><p>The smart film glass market is segmented into three main types: Electrochromic Smart Glass, which changes transparency based on electrical charge, allowing for dynamic control of light and heat; SPD Smart Glass, which uses suspended particle devices to transition from transparent to opaque with applied voltage, offering privacy and glare reduction; and PDLC Smart Glass, which toggles between translucent and transparent states when voltage is applied, providing versatility in privacy and aesthetic applications. Each type caters to different energy efficiency and privacy needs.</p></p>
