Slag-based aggregates originate from metal production processes, utilizing byproducts like steel or cast iron slag. Ash-based aggregates are derived from the combustion of materials like coal, offering lightweight and environmentally friendly alternatives. Other types may include recycled concrete or glass aggregates, promoting sustainable construction practices by reducing landfill waste and minimizing the need for virgin materials.</p></p>
