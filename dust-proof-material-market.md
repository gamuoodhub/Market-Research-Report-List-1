<p><p>The dust-proof material market includes various types such as polyester fabric, polypropylene (PP), polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE), aramid, and polyphenylene sulfide (PPS). Polyester fabric is durable, lightweight, and cost-effective, ideal for general dust protection. PP offers chemical resistance and moisture barrier properties, suitable for industrial applications. PTFE is highly resistant to heat and chemicals, used in extreme environments. Aramid provides high strength and heat resistance, often used in protective gear. PPS excels in thermal stability and chemical resistance, making it suitable for high-performance filtration systems. Each material caters to specific industrial and environmental needs.</p></p>
