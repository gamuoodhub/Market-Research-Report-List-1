<p><ul><li>Floating Execution</li><li>Fixed Execution</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surface aerator market is categorized into two main types: floating and fixed execution. Floating surface aerators are designed to move with the water's surface, providing flexibility and efficiency in various water bodies, often utilized in larger or deeper ponds. In contrast, fixed surface aerators are stationary installations, permanently mounted to the water surface, ideal for smaller or controlled environments. Both types aim to enhance oxygen transfer, improve water quality, and support aquatic life in wastewater treatment and agriculture applications.</p></p>
