<p><p>Weld fume extractors are categorized based on their operator capacity. Single operator extractors are designed for individual welding setups, efficiently capturing fumes generated by one welder in small to mid-sized operations. Double operator extractors accommodate two welders simultaneously, making them suitable for larger workspaces where partnership welding occurs. Multi-operator extractors serve extensive environments with numerous welders, providing robust filtration and airflow to manage significant fume volumes, ensuring safety and compliance in high-output manufacturing settings.</p></p>
