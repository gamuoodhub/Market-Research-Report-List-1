<p><p>The food service packaging market can be categorized into flexible and rigid packaging. Flexible packaging includes materials like plastic films and pouches that can easily conform to various shapes, providing convenience and reducing waste. It's often lightweight and can be resealable, enhancing its usability. In contrast, rigid packaging comprises containers such as boxes, jars, and cans that offer structural integrity and protection for food items. Rigid packaging is ideal for preserving freshness and is commonly used for bulk items, ensuring safe transport and storage.</p></p>
