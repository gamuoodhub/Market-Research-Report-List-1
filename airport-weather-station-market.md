<p><p>The Airport Weather Station market is segmented into two main types: Embedded Airport Weather Stations and Portable Airport Weather Stations. Embedded Airport Weather Stations are permanently installed at airports, providing continuous, real-time weather monitoring and data collection for operational safety and efficiency. In contrast, Portable Airport Weather Stations are mobile units that can be easily transported and set up in various locations, offering flexibility for temporary assessments or remote sites. Both types enhance decision-making in aviation operations through accurate weather information.</p></p>
