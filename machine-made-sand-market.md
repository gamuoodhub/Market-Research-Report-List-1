<p><p>The machine-made sand market comprises various types categorized by particle size. Coarse sand (Mx=3.7-3.1) features larger grains and is ideal for concrete and aggregate production. Medium sand (Mx=3.0-2.3) balances coarseness and fines, suitable for construction applications. Fine sand (Mx=2.2-1.6) serves in plastering and filler roles due to its smoother texture. Extra fine sand (Mx=1.5-0.7) consists of the smallest particles, commonly used in specialized applications like ceramics and glass making, ensuring high finesse and detail.</p></p>
