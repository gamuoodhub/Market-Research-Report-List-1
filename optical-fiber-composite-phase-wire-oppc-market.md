<p><strong>The Optical Fiber Composite Phase Wire (OPPC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Central Tube Structure OPPC</li><li>Layer Stranding Structure OPPC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Fiber Composite Phase Wire (OPPC) is a hybrid cable that combines optical fibers with traditional phase conductors for enhanced communication and electrical transmission. The Central Tube Structure OPPC features a central dielectric tube housing the optical fibers, providing optimal protection and stability. In contrast, the Layer Stranding Structure OPPC consists of optical fibers layered around a central conductor, offering flexibility and easier installation. Both types serve utilities, enabling efficient data transmission alongside power delivery in transmission lines.</p></p>
