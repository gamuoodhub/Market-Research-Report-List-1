<p><p>The Stone Gypsum market is primarily categorized into Natural Type and FGD (Flue Gas Desulfurization) Type. Natural gypsum is mined directly from geological deposits and is typically used in construction materials, agriculture, and manufacturing. FGD gypsum, on the other hand, is a byproduct of industrial processes aimed at reducing sulfur emissions from power plants. It is increasingly used as a sustainable alternative in construction and agriculture, leveraging its similar properties to natural gypsum while promoting environmental benefits.</p></p>
