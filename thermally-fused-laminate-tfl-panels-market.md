<p><p>Thermally Fused Laminate (TFL) panels are composite materials created by fusing a decorative layer of film to a substrate using heat and pressure. TFL panels are known for their versatility, offering a wide range of colors, textures, and finishes that can simulate the appearance of wood, stone, or other materials.</p><p>The TFL panels market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for lightweight, cost-effective, and aesthetically appealing building materials.
