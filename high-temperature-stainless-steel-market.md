<p><ul><li>High Temperature Austenitic Grades</li><li>High Temperature Ferritic Grades</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-temperature stainless steel market is primarily categorized into austenitic and ferritic grades. High-temperature austenitic grades are known for their excellent oxidation resistance, high strength, and ductility, making them suitable for extreme environments like power plants and chemical processing. In contrast, high-temperature ferritic grades offer good resistance to thermal fatigue and oxidation at elevated temperatures, albeit with lower ductility. Both types cater to industries where durability and performance under heat are critical, influencing their respective market dynamics.</p></p>
