<p><strong>The Extrusion Machinery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Screw Extrusion</li><li>Twin Screw Extrusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The extrusion machinery market features two primary types: single screw and twin screw extrusion. Single screw extrusion involves a single screw that conveys and melts plastic or other materials, making it suitable for simpler products and processes. In contrast, twin screw extrusion utilizes two intermeshing screws, allowing for better mixing, melting, and control over material properties, making it ideal for complex formulations and polymer blends. Both types cater to different production needs and applications in various industries.</p></p>
