<p><ul><li>Primary Treatment</li><li>Secondary Treatment</li><li>Multi Treatment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Produced water treatment systems are categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Primary Treatment** involves initial processes like sedimentation and filtration to remove larger solids and debris, reducing the load on subsequent treatments. </p><p>2. **Secondary Treatment** focuses on more advanced methods such as biological treatment and chemical processes that target dissolved contaminants, enhancing water quality further.</p><p>3. **Multi Treatment** combines various treatment technologies, integrating primary and secondary methods to efficiently handle diverse contaminants, optimizing overall water management and reuse in industrial applications.</p></p>
