<p><ul><li>Rising Spindle Penstock</li><li>Non-rising Spindle Penstock</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The penstock market includes two main types: Rising Spindle Penstocks and Non-rising Spindle Penstocks. Rising Spindle Penstocks feature a spindle that moves upward as the valve opens, providing visual confirmation of the valve position. They are commonly used in applications requiring precise flow control. Non-rising Spindle Penstocks have a fixed spindle, making them compact and suitable for confined spaces. Both types are essential in water management systems, with selection depending on space, operational needs, and maintenance requirements.</p></p>
